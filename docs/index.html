HydroBot, developed by the Mindcraft team, is a robot designed to tackle pollution and educate children about environmental conservation.

Who is HydroBot?

HydroBot, developed by the Mindcraft team, tackles pollution and educates children on conservation using 3D printing for efficiency and sustainability, inspiring environmental awareness.

Problematic

Pollution is a global crisis harming air, water, and ecosystems, fueling climate change, and threatening health and biodiversity. Its widespread impact demands urgent action for the protection of the planet and future generations.

Solution

Ocean cleanup robots are autonomous systems designed to remove pollutants like plastic and chemicals from water, covering large areas with minimal human intervention. They aid in reducing pollution, protecting marine life, and supporting ecosystem restoration.

Features

Solar Panel

Green and clean source of energy using solar Panel!

Artificial intelligence

Real time image processing with artificial Intelligence using python openCV 2!

Trash collecting

Our robot have a big net so it can easily collect all the trash around.

Open Source

HydroBot is 100% open source.
